Welcome to my website!

I am currently a second-year doctoral student at the Leiden University Centre for Linguistics (LUCL) in the Netherlands. I received my BA in English from Nankai University and MA in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics from Guangdong University of Foreign Studies. I am interested in theoretical syntax and Chinese grammar. My doctoral research focuses on phenomena related to the clausal periphery (e.g., sentence-final particles) in Chinese.

我是荷兰莱顿大学语言学中心二年级博士生，本科毕业于南开大学英语专业，硕士毕业于广东外语外贸大学外国语言学及应用语言学专业。我的研究兴趣主要是理论句法和现代汉语语法。我的博士研究课题主要关注与汉语句子边缘结构有关的现象，如句末助词。
