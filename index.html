About

Professional

I am currently a second-year doctoral student at the Leiden University Centre for Linguistics (LUCL) in the Netherlands.

I obtained my BA in English from Nankai University and my MA in Linguistics and Applied Linguistics from Guangdong University of Foreign Studies.

My research looks at issues in Chinese syntax within the generative framework, with a particular focus on phenomena related to the clausal periphery (e.g., sentence-final particles).
